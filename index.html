ABOUT US - WHO WE ARE

With a background in music theory and as a violinist, he began his journey into music production at 16 and trained as a sound engineer at Microfusa Barcelona.

Under the name Metaphysical, he signed with Nutek Records and worked as a resident DJ and event organizer for Bassground BCN and Munay Festival.

He has contributed as a sound designer for art and technology festivals with Ledscontrol and Superlative Live and as a music producer for independent artists, theater, advertising, and companies such as EDB, Antelope Audio, and Netflix Spain.

He is currently the sub-director at Guisla Studio, a studio that boasts a platinum record and over 100M streams, sponsored by Antelope Audio and Audialab.
